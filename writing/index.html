Writing

Before I made films about folk horror I spent fifteen years writing about it — and about the occult revival, hauntology, colonial violence in the landscape, and the filmmakers who got there first. The criticism below is not a separate career. It is the reading list behind the films.

Published for Sight & Sound, Frieze, The Wire and ArtReview, variously bylined Nate, Nathan and Nathaniel Budzinski.

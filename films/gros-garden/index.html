Gro's Garden

Feature Film  ·  Folk Horror
Denmark / United Kingdom  ·  2026

In post-production

Synopsis

Toby Harper is a plant scientist working at the edge of what he knows. He's moved to Denmark to find and study rare botanical specimens emerging in the ancient Scandinavian boglands due to climate change — methodical, driven, convinced that the landscape holds answers to questions he's spent a career forming.

The landscape has other ideas. Over the course of the film, Toby is drawn deeper into the fields and wetlands, his certainties dissolving at roughly the same rate as the boundary between research and obsession. What begins as a study of plants becomes a study of what grows in the swamplands of rational thought.
